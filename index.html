About Us

Our Beginning

The company's team has been producing furniture since 1992. Having started our activity from the production of doors, windows and outdoor furniture, we gradually moved to the production of furniture. Initially, all products were produced for the local market.

Our Expansion

With the growth of the quality and variety of products and the approach of the enlargement of the European Union, new opportunities opened up. In order to enter the western market, expand production areas, increase capacity we decided to create a new company - Home Styles, which started supplying its products to customers in Germany, Sweden, Denmark, England, Norway and the Netherlands.

Our Goal

The goal of "Home Styles" company is to produce high quality and various design furniture from various types of solid wood, maximally satisfying the customer's needs and quality. We can also offer flexible production, attractive price and ensure a competitive advantage.
